BloxFruits Races & Abilities

BloxFruits Races & Abilities

Human

V1 Buff: Increases base regeneration and slightly boosts running speed.

V2 Buff: Reduces cooldowns on all abilities by 10%.

V3 Buff: Unlocks "Last Resort," which increases damage based on missing health.

V4 Ability: Boosts overall damage and adds a powerful ability that increases mobility and attack power.

Fishman

V1 Buff: Reduces damage taken while in water.

V2 Buff: Increases swimming speed and damage reduction.

V3 Buff: Unlocks "Water Body," reducing damage taken by 90% for a short time.

V4 Ability: Grants full water immunity, massive damage reduction, and enhanced underwater combat abilities.

Sky

V1 Buff: Slower energy consumption while jumping and gliding.

V2 Buff: Increases energy regeneration and jump height.

V3 Buff: Unlocks "Heavenly Blood," restoring a percentage of health every second.

V4 Ability: Adds an air-based mobility skill and enhances energy regeneration significantly.

Mink

V1 Buff: Increased movement speed.

V2 Buff: Further boosts speed and dash range.

V3 Buff: Unlocks "Agility," granting a massive temporary speed boost.

V4 Ability: Adds lightning-based attacks and greatly enhances mobility.

Ghoul

V1 Buff: Life steal from melee attacks and increased regeneration at night.

V2 Buff: Further increases life steal and health regeneration.

V3 Buff: Unlocks "Cannibalism," granting a burst of healing upon damaging enemies.

V4 Ability: Dramatically boosts life steal and adds fear-based abilities.

Cyborg

V1 Buff: Reduces damage from ranged attacks.

V2 Buff: Further increases damage reduction and energy regeneration.

V3 Buff: Unlocks "Energy Core," releasing an explosion that deals damage and stuns enemies.

V4 Ability: Boosts defensive and offensive capabilities, along with enhanced energy bursts.

Rabbit

V1 Buff: Faster movement speed.

V2 Buff: Increases jump height and movement speed.

V3 Buff: Unlocks "Hop Blitz," giving powerful jump attacks and mobility.

V4 Ability: Amplifies speed-based attacks and adds a unique air dash ability.

BloxFruits Abilities

BloxFruits Abilities

Observation Haki (Ken Haki)

Description: Enhances dodging abilities and allows you to sense nearby enemies.

How to Unlock: Obtain 5,000 experience points in Observation from fighting NPCs.

Abilities: Enables auto-dodging up to a specific limit, increases awareness of enemy movements.

Armament Haki (Buso Haki)

Description: Enhances melee, sword, and gun attacks while allowing you to bypass Logia immunity.

How to Unlock: Purchase it from the Ability Teacher for 25,000 Beli.

Abilities: Boosts attack damage, applies a black coating to weapons and body.

Fruit Awakenings

Description: Upgrades specific fruits with powerful new abilities.

How to Unlock: Complete Raids to earn Fragments, then awaken each move.

Examples:

  • Flame: "Fire Flight" upgrade to "Rocket Flight."
  • Light: Adds new dash and stronger sword slashes.
  • Dough: Unlocks powerful AoE attacks and combos.

Flash Step

Description: A teleportation ability to quickly close the gap or dodge attacks.

How to Unlock: Purchase it from the Ability Teacher for 100,000 Beli.

Abilities: Allows short-range teleportation in any direction.

Enhanced Reflexes

Description: Boosts overall agility and jump height for easier mobility.

How to Unlock: Earn through specific quests or races like Sky.

Abilities: Increased jump height and decreased stamina consumption.

Water Kung Fu

Description: A water-based combat style with quick punches and mobility.

How to Unlock: Buy from the Water Kung Fu teacher for 750,000 Beli.

Abilities: Includes fast combos, area damage, and water-related attacks.

Electric Combat

Description: A fast melee fighting style that adds electric damage to attacks.

How to Unlock: Buy from the Mad Scientist for 500,000 Beli.

Abilities: Stuns enemies, deals consistent electric-based damage.

Sharkman Karate

Description: An advanced water-based fighting style with high damage.

How to Unlock: Complete the quest from Daigrock the Sharkman and pay 2.5 million Beli.

Abilities: Powerful water bursts and quick punches.
